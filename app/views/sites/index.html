Fetch the Paper is a mobile web app that makes it easier to share and save print articles. Take a photo of a magazine or newspaper article, then we'll find the online version and send you the link.

The benefits of digital reading, but less digital

1. Take photo of print article

Visit this website on your mobile phone and take a photo of a print article whose online version you want to save or share.

2. Enter your email

We'll find your article and email you the URL. Sign in through Facebook and we'll also make it easy to share and save searches.

3. Carry on screen-free

Spend less time looking at screens and more time enjoying your print newspapers and magazines!

Tools that enhance print

We love print. We think people spend too much time staring at screens. We can't imagine reading the Sunday morning paper on a tablet. We hope those that predict print's demise get assimilated by the Borg.

Still, digital has its advantages and we aim to bridge that gap.

Why print will never die

Eye strain cases are up 4000% in the last 10 years. Our eyes need print.

Unfolded, a newspaper spread has more viewing area than the largest computer screen, yet folds to the size of a tablet and costs a fraction of the amount.

When your 6mo old pukes all over you, would you rather be holding a digital device or paper magazine? Same question for when your 4yr old dumps apple juice on you.

To date, Warren Buffet has purchased 157 newspapers. Jeff Bezos bought the Washington Post. Good enough for Warren and Jeff, good enough for us.
